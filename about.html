Visa Urala

Digital Fashion Specialist / 3D Artist / Game Developer

Based in Helsinki, Finland

—

Specializing in game-ready character outfits and digital textile assets, I blend contemporary fashion with immersive world-building and hand-painted digital fabrics to create striking, atmospheric designs.

—

Across different media, I find myself drawn to oppressive atmospheres, immersive world-building, strong character silhouettes, distressed fabrics, fashion, and unique soundscapes.

Currently working as Lead Creative Director & Art Lead at False Member game studio, where I drive visual storytelling through character design, environment aesthetics, and mood-driven art direction. Beyond games, I compose contemporary music and experiment with various forms of digital art.

—

Inspired by: Hidetaka Miyazaki, David Lynch, Masamune Shirow, Alfred Pietroni, Robert Eggers, Ari Aster, Ville Kallio, and Viktor Antonov
